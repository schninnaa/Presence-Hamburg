Between the Volksparkstadion and the Barclays Arena lies a place where sports, music, and community come together. This open area offers space to meet, move, and enjoy special moments – whether before kickoff, between concerts, or simply while passing through.

On event days, it transforms into a vibrant meeting point for fans and visitors from all over the world. Outside of major events, it becomes a calm, open spot to pause and take in the view of two of Hamburg’s most iconic venues.

Here, urban life meets shared experiences – a place that captures the pulse and diversity of Hamburg.
