This digital installation explores the transformation of a public space through human presence, using the roundabout area between the Volksparkstadion and Barclays Arena in Hamburg as it’s prime example: a place that comes alive only when people gather for concerts or football matches. When the crowd leaves, the space turns quiet and grey, stripped of its energy and color. Through AR, this project visualizes how the atmosphere of a place depends on human presence, and how emptiness can reveal the contrast within a city that constantly moves between excitement and stillness.
